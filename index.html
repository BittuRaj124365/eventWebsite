TechConnect-2025

Atma Ram Sanatan Dharma College

University Of Delhi

The much-anticipated TechConnect 2025 event is here to bridge the gap between innovation and collaboration. Scheduled to take place on February 2025, at the iconic Atma Ram Sanatan Dharma College campus, this event promises an engaging experience for tech enthusiasts, professionals, and students. TechConnect 2025 will feature a blend of inspiring keynote speeches, interactive workshops, and networking opportunities designed to foster creativity and share knowledge. Renowned industry experts will deliver talks on emerging trends like Artificial Intelligence, Web 3.0, and Cybersecurity,offering attendees valuable insights into the future of technology.

Participants will have the opportunity to showcase their skills through hands-on coding competitions and project exhibitions, making this event an ideal platform for budding innovators to demonstrate their talent. The event also includes a panel discussion on the challenges and opportunities in today’s digital landscape, ensuring a well-rounded experience for all attendees. A registration form is available on the official event website, enabling participants to provide their preferences for sessions and workshops. A feedback section will be available post-event, allowing participants to share their thoughts and contribute to future improvements.

This year, TechConnect 2025 introduces exciting new features like a startup pitch contest, where aspiring entrepreneurs can present their ideas to a panel of investors, and a mentorship corner, offering one-on-one guidance from industry veterans. Attendees will also have access to exclusive hands-on workshops on advanced topics like Blockchain Development and Ethical Hacking, ensuring practical learning experiences. The venue will host a food court with a variety of cuisines to keep the energy high throughout the event.

Whether you’re looking to expand your professional network, learn from industry leaders, or gain hands-on experience, TechConnect 2025 offers something for everyone. The venue will also feature an exhibition zone where tech startups can display their latest products, providing a glimpse into cutting-edge innovations. Join us at TechConnect 2025 to ignite your passion for technology, meet like-minded individuals, and take your knowledge to the next level. Be part of this transformative journey where ideas meet execution and innovation comes to life. Mark your calendar for February 20, 2025— don’t miss out on this extraordinary event!


Date:- 20th feb, 2025

Time:- 11am to 5pm

Venue:- ARSD College Campus

Event Instructions for TechConnect 2025

1. Mandatory College ID Card: All participants must carry their college ID cards for verification at the registration desk.

2. Dress Code: Business casual attire is recommended to maintain a professional environment during the event.

3. Registration Confirmation: Please bring a digital or printed copy of your registration confirmation email for a seamless check-in process.

4. Punctuality: Attendees are requested to arrive 30 minutes before the event start time to avoid delays in the schedule.

5. Punctuality: A feedback form will be shared post-event. We encourage all participants to provide their valuable insights to help us improve future events.

These instructions ensure smooth coordination and an engaging experience for all attendees.
